# Refund Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-07-04

This policy explains when and how refunds are available for purchases made on skillz.ai. It applies to all
buyers, alongside our Terms of Service.

## 1. Scope

This policy covers three kinds of purchases on the platform: one-time dollar purchases, credit purchases, and
recurring subscriptions. Each is handled differently, as described below.

## 2. Dollar purchases

Items priced and paid for in dollars (e.g., avatar/voice commercial licenses, seller-priced models, RAG packs,
eval suites, asset packs, large/XL datasets) are eligible for a refund within **14 days** of purchase if the
product is **materially defective** (does not function as described, is corrupted, or fails our security scan
post-purchase) or **materially misrepresented** (substantially different from its listing).

- Refund requests outside this 14-day window, or for reasons other than defect/misrepresentation (e.g., change
  of mind, buyer's own compatibility issues), are handled at our discretion and are not guaranteed.
- If a refund is approved, the associated **license is revoked** and the item is removed from your account. You
  may not continue to use, host, or redistribute a product after its license has been revoked.

## 3. Credit purchases

Items priced in credits (e.g., skills, prompt packs, workflows, standard datasets, personal-use avatar/voice
packs) are licensed against your subscription's credit balance rather than charged directly.

- Credit purchases may be refunded **to your credit balance** at the platform's discretion, generally for the
  same defect-or-misrepresentation reasons described above for dollar purchases.
- Refunded credits are not convertible to cash and can only be used for future purchases on the platform.
- As with dollar purchases, an approved refund revokes the item's license.

## 4. Subscriptions

Basic, Pro, and Developer subscriptions can be **cancelled at any time** from your billing page.

- Cancelling stops all **future** billing — you will not be charged again after your current period ends.
- There is **no proration** for the remainder of a billing period already paid for.
- You retain access to your credit-licensed items through the end of the period you've already paid for. Once
  the period ends without renewal, credit-licensed items **deactivate** until you reactivate a plan.
- Items you purchased outright in dollars remain yours permanently and are unaffected by subscription
  cancellation.

## 5. How to request a refund

Email **support@skillz.ai** with your account email, the order or product in question, and the reason for your
request. Include any relevant detail (error messages, screenshots, a description of how the product differs
from its listing) so we can evaluate it quickly. We aim to respond to all refund requests within a few business
days.

## 6. Chargebacks

Please contact us before filing a chargeback with your bank or card issuer — most issues can be resolved faster
directly. Filing a chargeback without first contacting support may result in suspension of your account while
the dispute is investigated, and may affect your ability to use the platform going forward.

## 7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective date at
the top of this document. Continued use of skillz.ai after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the
revised policy.
