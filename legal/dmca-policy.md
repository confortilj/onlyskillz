# skillz.ai — DMCA & Anti-Piracy Policy (DRAFT — requires attorney review)

## For Designers: How We Protect Your Work
1. **Forensic fingerprinting.** Every copy of your Item delivered to any subscriber carries an invisible, account-unique identifier. The issuance registry records who received which copy, when, and from what IP.
2. **Leak tracing.** If your content appears anywhere it shouldn't, send us the leaked file or text. Our decoder identifies the source account in minutes (admin endpoint: `decode-fingerprint`).
3. **Evidence package.** For confirmed leaks we provide: the decoded fingerprint, the registry record (account, timestamp, IP, user agent), the artifact SHA-256, and the leaker's acceptance of the Terms — everything your attorney needs.
4. **Action.** skillz.ai terminates the leaking account, issues DMCA takedowns to the hosting platform, and cooperates with your legal action. Where skillz.ai holds enforcement rights under the Designer Agreement, we may pursue the claim directly.

## DMCA Takedown Notices (content ON skillz.ai)
If you believe content hosted on skillz.ai infringes your copyright, send a notice containing the elements required by 17 U.S.C. § 512(c)(3) to our designated agent:

> **DMCA Agent:** [NAME] · [EMAIL] · [ADDRESS]
> *(Register this agent with the U.S. Copyright Office at dmca.copyright.gov — $6 fee — before launch.)*

We will remove or disable access to allegedly infringing material expeditiously, notify the uploader, and accept counter-notifications per § 512(g). Repeat infringers are terminated.

## Filing Takedowns FOR Designers (content leaked OFF-platform)
On request, skillz.ai files takedown notices on a designer's behalf with GitHub, Hugging Face, Discord, Google, and file-sharing hosts, attaching the fingerprint evidence package.
