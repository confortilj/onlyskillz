Seller guide

How to list, price, and sell your work on skillz.ai — and get paid for it.

Becoming a seller

Any account can become a seller. Open the upload flow from your dashboard, connect a Stripe Connect account for payouts, and submit your first product. Every submission goes through an automated security scan before it can go live — see below for what that checks.

Preparing your product

Accepted file formats depend on product type:

Product type Accepted formats
Skills, prompt packs.md or .zip
Datasets, RAG packs.csv, .jsonl, or .zip
Models.safetensors or .gguf
Voices.wav or .mp3
Avatars.mp4 or .zip
Asset packs.zip or .svg

Size limits are set per product type: skills, prompt packs & workflows 25 MB · eval suites 500 MB · voices 5 GB · RAG & asset packs 10 GB · models & avatars 20 GB · datasets by tier (small 100 MB · medium 1 GB · large 10 GB · XL 50 GB). Files up to 10 MB upload inline; anything larger uploads directly to secure storage — keep the tab open until it finishes.

Good docs sell. Write a clear overview of what your product does, concrete usage instructions, and 1-2 worked examples showing real input/output. Buyers are far more likely to purchase — and less likely to request a refund — when they know exactly what they're getting before they buy.

The security scan

Every upload is automatically scanned before publishing. A static analysis engine checks for malicious code patterns, data exfiltration attempts, dependency issues, and over-broad permission requests, tagging any findings with CWE identifiers. After publishing, a dynamic sandbox continues to monitor behavior.

Score Outcome
≥ 90"Verified Safe" badge, publishes automatically
70–89Publishes without the badge
< 70Blocked from publishing
Sensitive findingsRouted to manual review regardless of score

Pricing your product

Some product types are auto-priced in credits — you don't set the price, the platform does:

  • Skills — ⬡5 credits
  • Prompt packs — ⬡2 credits
  • Workflows — ⬡30 credits
  • Datasets (small) — ⬡15 credits, (medium) — ⬡30 credits

Other types are seller-priced in dollars — you choose the price:

  • Models (can be listed free)
  • RAG packs, eval suites, asset packs
  • Large and XL datasets
  • Avatar / voice commercial licenses

Avatars and voices sell in two tiers: a fixed ⬡60-credit personal-use pack, and a commercial license that you price in dollars yourself. Commercial licenses are perpetual — they survive the buyer cancelling their subscription.

For datasets, choose the size tier that matches your data honestly — small and medium are fixed-credit, large and XL let you set a dollar price. Mis-tiering a dataset to dodge pricing is a ToS violation.

Revenue split & payouts

skillz.ai keeps 33% of every transaction; you keep 67%. Payouts run monthly via Stripe Connect. For credit-based sales, each credit is valued at $0.50 when your payout is calculated.

You'll need to complete Stripe Connect onboarding before payouts can be sent to you — start this as soon as you register as a seller so you're not waiting on it after your first sale.

Watermarking & anti-piracy protection

Every file a buyer downloads is automatically fingerprinted to them — zero-width text marks, dataset canary rows, audio LSB marks, MP4 metadata and frame-level marks, safetensors metadata, or SVG marks, depending on format. A daily crawler scans for leaked copies of marketplace products across the web, and any match is traced back to the buyer who leaked it. This protection is automatic — you don't need to do anything to enable it.

Unpublishing

You can unpublish your own product at any time from your seller dashboard. Unpublishing removes it from the catalog for new buyers; existing buyers keep the license and access they already have (per the terms above — credit-licensed items still depend on their subscription staying active).

Rules

No malware, obfuscated code, data exfiltration, or undisclosed network calls. No infringing content — you must own or have the rights to everything you upload, including any training data, voice likeness, or avatar likeness.

If you believe a listed product infringes your rights, send a DMCA takedown notice to support@skillz.ai with a description of the work, the infringing listing's URL, and a statement of good faith belief and accuracy under penalty of perjury. We'll review and take down qualifying content promptly, and notify the seller per our DMCA policy.

License verification API

Every purchase issues a unique license key, and subscription-bound licenses deactivate automatically when the buyer's plan lapses. You can enforce this in your own product by checking the key at load time — no auth needed; the key itself is the secret: 

POST https://mkofedsnuzyflblwhdrs.supabase.co/functions/v1/verify-license
Content-Type: application/json

{"license_key": "sk-live-XXXXXXXX-XXXXXXXX-XXXXXXXX"}

→ {"valid": true, "status": "active", "kind": "credit_purchase",
   "product_id": "your-product-id", "checked_at": "…"}

valid is false for revoked, refunded, or lapsed licenses. Cache results for up to an hour to be kind to the API.
