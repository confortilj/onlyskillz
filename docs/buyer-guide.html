Buyer guide

Everything you need to know about plans, credits, downloads, and licenses on skillz.ai.

How plans & credits work

skillz.ai runs on a monthly subscription. Your plan gives you a pool of credits that refill every billing cycle, plus access to certain product types. Most catalog items — skills, prompt packs, models, workflows, and standard datasets — are priced in credits. Higher‑end items — models with custom weights, RAG packs, eval suites, asset packs, avatar/voice commercial licenses, and large datasets — are priced by the seller in dollars instead.

Unused credits do not roll over between billing periods. Annual billing gets you 20% off the monthly price on any plan.

Buying with credits vs. dollars

When an item's price shows a ⬡ credit amount, checkout simply deducts credits from your monthly balance — no card charge. When an item shows a $ price, you're charged that amount directly (in addition to your subscription) and the item is yours to keep regardless of what happens to your subscription later.

Out of credits mid‑month? You can buy extra credits at $0.50 each, sold in packs of 20 ($10), 60 ($30), or 120 ($60), from your billing page.

Downloads & watermarking

There's no separate installer or CLI tool — once you've licensed an item, its product page button turns into your download. Just click it to get your copy.

Every file you download is forensically watermarked and fingerprinted to your account. Depending on the format, this can mean zero‑width text marks, canary rows hidden in datasets, least-significant-bit audio marks, MP4 metadata and frame-level marks, safetensors metadata, or SVG marks. These marks are invisible in normal use but make any leaked copy traceable back to the original buyer.

A daily crawler scans public sources for leaked copies of marketplace items. Redistributing or leaking a purchased item outside the terms of your license is a breach of our Terms of Service and may result in account action.

License terms: subscription-bound vs. perpetual

Credit-licensed items (skills, prompts, models, workflows, datasets, avatar/voice personal packs, etc.) are tied to an active subscription. As long as you're subscribed, you keep full access.

Dollar-purchased items (commercial avatar/voice licenses, seller-priced models, RAG packs, eval suites, asset packs, large datasets) are perpetual — once purchased, they're yours to keep and use even if you later cancel your subscription.

What happens if your subscription lapses

If your subscription lapses — payment fails or you cancel — credit-licensed items deactivate and become unavailable for download or use until you reactivate a plan. Anything you bought outright with dollars stays fully licensed and downloadable, subscription or not.

Refunds

Dollar purchases are refundable within 14 days if the product is materially defective or misrepresented. Approved refunds revoke the associated license.

Credit purchases are refunded to your credit balance at the platform's discretion, generally for the same defective-or-misrepresented reasons.

Subscriptions can be cancelled any time. Cancelling stops future billing — there's no proration for the current period, and you keep access to credit-licensed items until the period ends.
